Manoj Pant


An expert in International Trade and professor at JNU, Manoj Pant is also, among other things, a lead speaker on WTO issues, an expert member of the consultative group on trade issues of the Planning Commission and a member of the technical committee of the Ministry of Human Resource Development for WTO. Apart from writing a monthly column for The Economic Times for over a decade, he has published two books and over a dozen articles in national and international referred journals.
